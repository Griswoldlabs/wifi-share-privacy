WiFi Share - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Overview

WiFi Share ("the App") is a utility app that generates QR codes for WiFi networks. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

WiFi Share does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No analytics - We do not use any analytics or tracking services.
  • No network requests - The App does not connect to the internet (except for optional sharing features initiated by you).
  • No accounts - No sign-up or login is required.
  • No advertising - The App contains no ads or ad tracking.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Saved WiFi networks - Network names (SSIDs) and passwords you choose to save are stored in encrypted device storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain).
  • App preferences - Your settings (theme, QR style preferences) are stored locally.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or the App developer.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera - Used only to scan QR codes. Camera data is processed on-device and never recorded or transmitted.
  • Location (Android) - Required by Android to read the current WiFi network name. This is an Android system requirement. Location data is never stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

WiFi Share does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App does not collect any information from anyone.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete all App data:

  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in the App's Settings screen, or
  • Uninstall the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
